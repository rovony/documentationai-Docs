<Callout kind="info" title="Prerequisites">
Before starting, ensure you have:
- A web browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Safari recommended)
- Sample PK data ready (CSV format with columns for time, dose, concentration)
- No credit card needed for the free trial (3 messages included)
</Callout>

## Get Started in Minutes

Malek provides a chat-first workspace where you can run pharmacometric analyses like NCA and PopPK using natural language prompts. Follow these steps to analyze your first PK dataset.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up for Free Trial" icon="user-plus">

    Visit [app.malek.com/signup](https://app.malek.com/signup) and create your account.

    1. Enter your email and create a password.
    2. Verify your email via the confirmation link.
    3. Log in to access the dashboard.

    <Callout kind="tip">
      The free trial gives you 3 messages to test NCA, PopPK, or regulatory summaries.
    </Callout>

  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Your First Project" icon="folder-plus">

    From the dashboard, click **New Project**.

    - Name it `My First PK Analysis`.
    - Select **Pharmacometrics** workspace.
    - Click **Create**.

    This sets up your chat-first environment with 29 AI agents ready for NCA, PopPK, and more.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload Data and Run NCA" icon="upload">

    In your project chat:

    1. Click **Upload Data** and select your PK CSV file.
    2. Type: "Run NCA on my uploaded PK data for dose proportionality."

    Malek's NCA agent processes your time-concentration data and generates:

    - Key parameters: `AUC`, `Cmax`, `Tmax`
    - Diagnostic plots (spaghetti, forest)
    - Compliance checks

    Example sample data format:

    ````csv
    Subject,Dose,Time,Concentration
    001,100,0,0
    001,100,0.5,15.2
    001,100,1,25.8
    001,100,2,30.1
    002,200,0,0
    002,200,0.5,28.4
    002,200,1,45.6
    002,200,2,55.3
    ````

  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Results" icon="bar-chart">

    View interactive Plotly charts and download reports.

    - Export PDF for regulatory submission.
    - Share with team via project RBAC.
    - Iterate: Ask "Simulate higher dose regimens."

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Try Different Analyses

Switch between agents seamlessly in the chat workspace.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="NCA Analysis" icon="activity">

    Prompt: "Perform non-compartmental analysis on this PK data and check linearity."

    Expect: `AUCinf`, `CL/F`, lambda_z with plots.

  </Tab>

  <Tab title="PopPK Model" icon="trending-up">

    Prompt: "Build a 1-compartment PopPK model from my dataset."

    Expect: Parameter estimates, VPC simulation.

  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Regulatory Check" icon="shield">

    Prompt: "Summarize FDA guidance on NCA for this study."

    Expect: Inline citations, compliance flags.

  </Tab>
</Tabs>
