## Overview

Malek is the AI workbench designed for pharmaceutical sciences teams. You get access to 29 specialized AI agents across 8 departments, enabling fast pharmacometrics workflows. Handle non-compartmental analysis (NCA), population PK (PopPK) modeling, regulatory intelligence, publication-ready charts, and compliant automation—all in a chat-first interface.

Key benefits include accelerated analysis, regulatory compliance with 21 CFR Part 11 features like audit logs and electronic signatures, and seamless team collaboration with RBAC and shared knowledge bases.

## Quick Start

Get up and running with Malek in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Create your free account at `https://app.malek.com/signup`. No credit card required for the first 3 messages.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a Chat" icon="message-circle">
    Open the workspace and ask: "Run NCA on my PK data" or "Build a PopPK model".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Data" icon="upload">
    Share your CSV or PK dataset directly in chat for instant analysis.
  </Step>
