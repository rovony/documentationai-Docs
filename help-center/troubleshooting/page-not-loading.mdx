---
title: Dashboard Page Not Loading
description: Fix the Malek dashboard if it won't load.
---

## Symptoms
- Blank screen or endless spinner
- Error: "Failed to load resource"

## Causes
- Internet connection issues
- Browser cache problems

## Solution
<Steps>
  <Step title="Refresh the page">
    Try a hard refresh:
    ````bash
    Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows)
    Cmd+Shift+R (Mac)
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Clear cookies and cache, then log in again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check network">
    Ensure you are online and retry.
  </Step>
</Steps>
