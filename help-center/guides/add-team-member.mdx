title: Adding a Team Member
description: Invite others to collaborate on Malek projects.
---

## Overview

You can invite teammates to work together on your Malek documentation.

## Steps
<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your project">
    From your Malek dashboard, select your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Team Settings">
    Click "Team" in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite member">
    Enter the user's email and click "Send Invite".
  </Step>
  <Step title="New member joins">
    The user receives an email link and joins the project.
  </Step>
</Steps>
