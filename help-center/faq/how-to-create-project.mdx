## Overview

To organize your documentation, create a new project in Malek.

## Steps

1. Sign in to your Malek dashboard.
2. Click "New Project" at the top right.
3. Enter your project name and (optionally) a description.
4. Click "Create".
5. Start adding docs or invite team members.

Projects help keep your documentation organized by team, product, or topic.
